Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At ChromaPro ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension, ChromaPro: Professional Color Picker & Palette (the "Extension"), handles your data.

1. Data Collection

ChromaPro operates locally within your browser. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.

  • Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics tools tracking your usage within the extension.
  • Telemetry: We do not collect telemetry or crash reports.

2. Local Storage

The Extension uses the Chrome Local Storage API strictly to save your color history and preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. Permissions Explained

To function correctly, ChromaPro requires the following permissions:

  • storage: Required to save your recently picked colors and palette preferences locally on your device.
